Attributes
==========

STR - Strength
CON - Constitution
AGI - Agility
INT - Intelligence
PER - Perception (of surrounding environment)
MAG - Magic ability
CHA - Charm (ability to charm anybody)
APP - Appearance (physical attractiveness)
LUC - Luck

For any of these, 5000 is normal, although this number is divided by 50 when displayed to the player (thus showing 100).  Min stat is 0; max stat is 49999 (which results in a display of 999).

Experience levels
=================

experience needed to gain a level = ROUND(2 * (1.5 ^ current level), 0) * 10
